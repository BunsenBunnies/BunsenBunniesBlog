Message From Ann (Artist)

I hope you like the look of the new website! I am still tinkering away on it in the background so small details may continue to change over the upcoming days.

The last few pages of Issue 8 are in progress, the remaining pages have been sketched out and are being completed digitally so should be ready ASAP.

John and I have been working hard in the background to relaunch Bunsen Bunnies after the Year or so off, Getting married was brilliant yet very time consuming. We have discussed the planned schedule for the comic and have decided that in order to keep it running without the artist (me) cracking up, some changes would be needed. Firstly, will be posing completed pages as and when they complete, normally in batches of 5 or so at a time instead of the page a week schedule we ran previously. Secondly we are also working on other projects such as the Bugs and Boiled Eggs Meerkat series, so the Bunsen Bunnies issues may be interspersed with other tales when appropriate.

The Blog at the bottom of the page should update with details of progress and upcoming Comic pages reasonably regularily. Please feel free to submit Questions for both John and I to respond to here!

So please email in any comic related queries you have to creators@bunsenbunnies.co.uk
